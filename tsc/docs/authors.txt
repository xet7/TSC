List of TSC contributors & copyright holders
============================================

This file lists all people who have contributed to the Secret
Chronicles of Dr. M. or its former codebase, and hence hold copyright
on it. All names are shown in categorized alphabetical order.

Apart from this, code taken from other projects under specific
licenses is marked as such in the code. To find these, issue
this command from the "tsc" directory:

  $ grep -nr Copyright src | grep -v "TSC Contributors"
